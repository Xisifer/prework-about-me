Where I'm From

Originally, I hail from Silicon Valley in the California Bay Area (specifically Redwood City). In 2011 I moved out to Austin to begin working in the video game industry, where I still am today.

What are your favorite hobbies?

Oh, plenty of things. Video games primarily, but gaming functions as more of a socialization tool and a way to keep in contact with friends across the country, as well as a window and introduction to other hobbies (such as Dungeons & Dragons) which then feed into yet other sub-hobbies (such as creative writing). In the realm of sports, I also enjoyed sport fencing and archery in college, I just haven't managed to find the spare time and/or finances and/or commitment to seek out any classes for it yet.

What's your dream job?

I'm no longer certain. When I was a teenager, I thought becoming a video game developer was my Dream Job, but 8 years of Quality Assurance experience and a half-dozen game development studios later, I've discovered that the industry is not exactly the Wonka's Chocolate Factory I envisioned.

Why do you want to be a web developer?
